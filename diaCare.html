Diamond & Gemstone Jewelry Care

We're here to help keep your diamond & Gemstone shiny.

Day-to-day Care

It is not recommended to expose diamonds and jewelry to different temperatures, cosmetics and ultrasonic cleaning.

How to Clean Diamond & Gemstone Jewelry

1. Place your gemstone jewelry into the jewelry cleaner solution.

2. After two minutes, use a pair of tweezers to remove your jewelry from the solution.

3. Gently clean behind the settings and in between crevices with a soft bristle brush to remove any debris.

4. Rinse the jewelry and dry with a polishing cloth.
